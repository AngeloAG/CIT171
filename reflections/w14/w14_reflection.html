Why is this week's topic important? - 5

I think we talked about this on class last Wednesday, Synergy is the ultimate level of performance of a team, the ability of a team to achieve synergy demonstrates that they have mastered to some degree all aspects of the MIM model of influence, with all that hard work gathered together the whole team and its potential is far greater than the sum of its parts (in other words each member of the team). And because of that, each person that wants a successful future has to learn the importance of synergy and have it as his/her main goal when entering a new team.

What did I learn from the study material? - 5

That synergy cannot be achieved from one day to the other (in some sense). There has to be work from all the members of the team to understand each other's motivations, fears, backgrounds, and goals, in order to achieve a consensus that will open the window to synergy to happen. Also, all parts must compromise to the common goal the team tries to achieve. It is good and positive to have differences between the team members, but the team must be careful to address the differences in the right way, as a way to improve and learn from each other rather than a source of conflict.

Why is this week's topic important for teamwork? - 20

Because every team should aim to achieve synergy. As we learned in the readings, synergy is what allows miracles to happen, it empowers individuals on a team to aim for more important things, to get excited about the work they are currently doing, and to give a 100% for the project the team is working on to succeed. Also, more than being just the goal, achieving synergy ensures that deep work has been done to gather each individual on the team to mutual understanding and a sense of respect and care for each other member of the team. In a sense, synergy helps us become better human beings at the end of the day.
