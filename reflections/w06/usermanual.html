Introduction

This manual is designed to help the user understand the behavior, preferences, and features of the present component “Alberto Angelo Arellano Gaona”; learn how it works, what features it has, and how to use it. It describes the overall style of work both in personal and job-related environments and relations; how it reacts to specific stimuli; its values; and how it processes information and makes decisions.

Disclaimer

This document is not designed as an intensive and/or extensive description of the component but as a general guide of usage and interaction with human and non-human beings in personal/work social relations and under personal/casual/work situations and environments.

From now on the component will be referred to in the first person.

My Style

It is a balance between personal and teamwork. In all settings no matter if it is a casual interaction or a job-related project my participation is guided by this balance. I make sure that the part I am supposed to do in the project/relation/interaction is completed in a proper and timely manner, and I enjoy working on it alone and at my own pace. Consequently, I expect and give the benefit of the same to all the participants. After this is done, I enjoy complementing my part with others’ work, giving, receiving, and modifying as appropriate for the benefit of the project/relation success.

I make things personal but I don’t take them personally. My interactions and participation in work projects or relationships are personal in the sense that I make them my own and care about them as something belonging to myself. However, I don’t take personally the interactions of the project (and its participants) towards myself. They are always considered under the scope of the project/relation at issue.

What I value

I value honesty and accountability as they reflect the level of perception of the reality an individual has and how conscious he/she is of the surroundings.

I value the true effort.

I love creativity and defend structure and defined procedures.

I balance the importance of feelings, beliefs, traditions, preferences, etc., and the efficiency, success, extent, etc., of a project (either job related or not).

What I don't have patience for

The unwillingness to acknowledge reality. I advocate in favor of dreams, principles, values, moral duties, preferences, desires, etc., but I am conscious of the circumstances surrounding an issue/project and how they interact with each other.

How best to communicate with me

A general rule of thumb.

Be direct, no further elaboration, presentation or social pre-contant, filtering, etc., is needed. However, be precise. I don’t generally make assumptions beyond what I am being informed about nor understand hidden hints in the conversation.

Expect questions. I use them as a tool to confirm that what I understand is what is being told to me.

How to help me

Usually, doing your part. The best way to help generally is to prevent me to intervene in other’s people work when possible. Of course, I enjoy helping others, but when it's done with malicious intent the help is often terminated with no further elaboration.

What people misunderstand about me

That I don’t care about their feelings. And I attribute it to the fact that I balance the health and success of any project/relation with the feelings and beliefs of the participants. I don’t let personal feelings ruin or break down a project or relationship but at the same time, I am aware of the inherent value each participant has. So, I don’t sacrifice either side, I try to keep the balance the best I can.

Personal Dashboard

Health

Define: 

For me, it is the level of well-being of a system, in this case, myself. Which includes, but is not limited to, performance, efficiency, durability, strength, and stability. It covers me as a whole and, my mind, spirit, and body individually. It is the basis for all other activities and goals. I often struggle with this point because I place work, family, and other activities before, which in the long term is counter-productive. That’s why I work constantly to make changes to it.

Measure:

60 / 100.

Analysis: 

There are some things I want to change and that I have been actively trying to improve. Nutrition, like many others I want to improve my diet. I don’t stick with a formal diet as others do. My goal is basic, three meals a day that share a close to even amount of carbohydrates, fat, and protein. Avoid snacks as much as possible. (I don’t list water intake because I have mastered that one).

Workout, as many people do. But I don’t like to go to Gyms, so my first goal is to be able to perform a simple routine of 15 minutes a day (no matter the time of the day).

Summary:

Yes, it is a gravity problem. It is necessary to have body-mind stability to perform successfully other activities.

Work

Define: 

The physical and mental actions of the individual. I often place this point ahead of health. It is not limited to only job-related work but also other areas like family, friendships, and formal/informal studies. I have been successful in completing these activities.

Measure:

80 / 100.

Analysis:

I would like to balance better the different fields in this topic. I often invest a great number of resources (not only monetary) in this field and that leaves me struggling more to successfully address the remaining ones. It is natural, of course, that a job takes more resources in normal life, but I want to improve the efficiency of that process to use my limited resources more wisely.

Summary:

Yes, it is a gravity problem. It is affecting negatively my health and it will not last that much.

Play

Define: 

It is not going as well as it could. Even though I enjoy my formal job and studies as if they were games, the pressure involved in them makes them drain more energy than they provide. Some play I enjoy goes from the board and video games to physical activities like archery and camping. Learning is the most important part for me. That is the main source of entertainment, second goes the social interactions that happen as a consequence of the game.

Measure:

40 / 100.

Analysis:

I am not spending a healthy amount of time and effort in non-work/study-related play. That is a design flaw I have been trying to address. As taking spare time had not worked I will need to determine specific moments to play and enjoy without thinking about work and studies.

Summary:

It is not critical. However, it has to be addressed because in the long run it can cause serious issues and interfere with the success of other goals.

Compass

My Workview

For me, work is just another type of entertainment with the implicit extra of giving me money which brings a subtle disadvantage, the pressure related to it makes it a little bit harder to enjoy it compared to other activities but it is not so overwhelming. I don’t see work as some sort of a dream or even related to it, it is more like a game with a set of rules and as pretty much any game has some kind of fun (in some sense). My purpose behind work is to distribute it evenly between entertainment and money. Even when it seems boring I am able to find entertainment in learning how it functions and how to master it.

My Lifeview

I think the most important thing I have in my possession is my free will. And I make a clear distinction between factors that influence my decisions and my decisions themselves. For that reason, I don’t feel compelled or “obligated” to do things because of a sense of responsibility, gratitude, compassion, mere desire, etc. And for me, what I value more is family and becoming more like Christ.

What I am trying to say is that I have tried to make my own will the inflection point of my decisions. Thus making me completely responsible for their outcomes.

That made me free. I stopped completely worrying about things I have no power to influence, and at the same time, engage in whatever I found important for me.

Workview - Lifeview Follow-up.

They complement each other pretty well. The only two problems I face are “time” and “energy”. Being both what limits me in my activities.

They are usually driven by a mix of my will and necessity. That’s why often trying to differentiate between the two is tricky.
