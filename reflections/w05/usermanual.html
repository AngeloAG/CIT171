Alberto Angelo Arellano Gaona User Manual.

Introduction

This manual is designed to help the user understand the behavior, preferences and features of the present component “Alberto Angelo Arellano Gaona”; learn how it works, what features does it has and how to use it. It describes the overall style of work both in personal and job related environments and relations; how it reacts to specific stimuli; its values; and how it processes information and makes decisions.

Disclaimer

This document is not designed as an intensive and/or extensive description of the component but as a general guide of usage and interaction with human and non-human beings in personal/work social relations and under personal/casual/work situations and environments.

From now on the component will be referred to in first person.

My Style

It is a balance between personal and team work. In all settings no matter if it is a casual interaction or a job related project my participation is guided by this balance. I make sure that the part I am supposed to do in the project/relation/interaction is completed in a proper and timely manner, and I enjoy working on it alone and at my own pace. Consequently, I expect and give the benefit of the same to all the participants. After this is done, I enjoy complementing my part with other’s work, giving, receiving and modifying as appropriate for the benefit of the project/relation success.

I make things personal but I don’t take them personal. My interactions and participation towards work projects or relationships are personal in the sense that I make them my own and care about them as something belonging to myself. However, I don’t take personal the interactions of the project (and its participants) towards myself. They are always considered under the scope of the project/relation at issue.

What I value

I value honesty and accountability as they reflect the level of perception of the reality an individual has and how conscious he/she is of the surroundings.

I value true effort.

I love creativity and defend structure and defined procedures.

I balance the importance of feelings, beliefs, traditions, preferences, etc., and the efficiency, success, extent, etc., of a project (either job related or not).

What I don't have patience for

The unwillingness to acknowledge reality. I advocate in favor of dreams, principles, values, moral duties, preferences, desires, etc., but I am conscious of the circumstances surrounding an issue/project and how they interact with peach other.

How best to communicate with me

A general rule of thumb.

Be direct, no further elaboration, presentation or social pre-contant, filtering, etc., is needed. However, be precise. I don’t generally make assumptions beyond what I am being informed about nor understand hidden hints in the conversation.

Expect questions. I use them as a tool to confirm that what I am understanding is what is being told to me.

How to help me

Usually, doing your part. The best way to help generally is preventing me to intervene in other’s people work when possible. Of course I enjoy to help others, but when its done with malicious intent the help is often terminated with no further elaboration.

What people misunderstand about me

That I don’t care about their feelings. And I attribute it to the fact that I balance the health and success of any project/relation with the feelings and beliefs of the participants. I don’t let personal feelings ruin or break down a project or relation but at the same time I am aware of the inherent value each participant has. So, I don’t sacrifice either side, I try to keep the balance the best I can.
