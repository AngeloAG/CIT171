Why is this week's topic important? - 5

Cooperation is key for a team to achieve any goal. Many problems humankind has faced cannot be solved by just one individual, and even if some could the time needed for just one person to achieve considerable progress would be several lifetimes of span. For that reason, individuals from different backgrounds and sets of skills collaborate together, this breaks up the individual workload and thus makes progress more attainable. Also, cooperation sets the path for specialization, as an individual is not required to understand the whole project or do it alone, he/she can focus on getting better at a few sets of skills.
