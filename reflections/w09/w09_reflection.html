Why is this week's topic important for teamwork? - 20

Because the fastest way to break a team is to break the rules that drive the team forward. All teams face problems, heavy loads, etc., but the team members often take those things and work them out for the well-being of the project at hand, and assume the other team members are doing the same, but when a person tries to take advantage over the others in an unethical way, that’s when the others don’t see a reason to continue the fair play, and that’s when chaos happens. So, the best way to have a team that works well and endures the pains of a project is to set a high ethical standard and enforce it to the best extent possible.
