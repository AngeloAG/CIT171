What did I learn from the study material - 5

That we are not good at making estimates. That’s something I noticed from the time I have been working as a developer. I am a witness to pretty much everything we read in the material of this week. The amount of uncertainty (especially in a startup like the one I work in) is just to much to make a precise estimate. Not only about the technologies, we plan to use, but with user requirements too. I have been in the situation of being almost done with the current spring only to find that the client didn’t want what they told us. And had to change everything again.

Why is this week's topic important? - 5

Because we will be doing estimates, and we have to know how to handle that situation. We just cannot avoid making estimates (there are pretty much vital) so we have to get good at least at giving some kind of estimates.
